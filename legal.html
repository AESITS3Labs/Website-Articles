Accessibility Statement▸

BACKGROUND
We are committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We continually improve the user experience for everyone and apply relevant accessibility standards to ensure equal access and opportunity for all users.

CONTENTS:
Our Commitment • Accessibility Features • Ongoing Improvement • Feedback and Support • Third-Party Content • Technical Specifications

1. Our Commitment

We strive to ensure our website conforms to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1, Level AA, which establishes standards for creating content that is perceivable, operable, understandable, and robust for all users.

Our accessibility efforts also align with:
Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA)
Section 508 of the U.S. Rehabilitation Act

2. Accessibility Features

Our website incorporates accessibility features designed to support users with diverse needs:

  • Assistive Technology Support: Compatibility with screen readers and other assistive devices
  • Image Accessibility: Alternative text descriptions for meaningful images
  • Navigation: Logical heading structures and ARIA landmarks for efficient navigation
  • Visual Design: High contrast ratios between text and background colors
  • Keyboard Navigation: Full keyboard accessibility for all interactive elements
  • Clear Communication: Descriptive link text and properly labeled form controls

3. Ongoing Improvement

We recognize accessibility as an ongoing commitment. Our team regularly:

  • Conducts accessibility audits and testing
  • Reviews and updates content for compliance
  • Implements user feedback and accessibility improvements
  • Provides staff training on accessibility best practices

4. Feedback and Support

We welcome your feedback on the accessibility of our website. If you encounter accessibility barriers or need assistance accessing our content, please get in touch with us:

Email: info@aesit.net
Phone: 1-571-480-1329

We aim to respond to accessibility inquiries within five (5) business days.

5. Third-Party Content

Our website may link to third-party websites or services that are not under our control. We cannot guarantee the accessibility of external content, but encourage all partners and vendors to maintain accessible digital practices.

6. Technical Specifications

This website is being re-designed to be accessible across various technologies and platforms, including:

  • Modern web browsers
  • Assistive technologies such as screen readers
  • Voice recognition software
  • Alternative input devices

2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, smartphone, tablet) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide information to website owners about user behavior and preferences.

Cookies Can Be —
Session cookies: Temporary cookies that are deleted when you close your browser
Persistent cookies: Cookies that remain on your device until they expire or are manually deleted
First-party cookies: Set directly by our website
Third-party cookies: Set by external services we use on our website
