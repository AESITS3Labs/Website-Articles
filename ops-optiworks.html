OptiWorks® & the CRELI Index™

OptiWorks® contributes to Flow Intelligence by structuring how space and services operate. Its capabilities are intended to support uplift in CRELI Index™ categories such as:

  • Operational Efficiency & Digital Twin Maturity — via integrated space and service data feeding operational models
  • Sustainability & Human Experience — via workplace strategies that improve comfort, utilization, and ESG outcomes
  • AI & Intelligence Encoding — via structured data that can be used for predictive workplace analytics

These are intended impact pathways. CRELI Index™ scoring is still being piloted; quantified score changes tied to OptiWorks® deployments will be added once pilot results are available.
