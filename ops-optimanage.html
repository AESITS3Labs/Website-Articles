OptiManage® & the CRELI Index™

OptiManage® is a core Flow Intelligence practice. Its EAM capabilities are designed to support uplift in CRELI Index™ categories such as:

  • Operational Efficiency & Digital Twin Maturity — via structured asset data and integrated work management
  • Financial Velocity™ — via reduced downtime, optimized maintenance, and clearer OPEX control
  • Lifecycle Data Continuity — via operational records that extend the design data thread

These are intended impact pathways, not yet validated score outcomes. CRELI Index™ scoring is in pilot; this page will be updated with quantified improvements once pilot data is available.
