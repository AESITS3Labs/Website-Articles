EcoFlow® & the CRELI Index™

EcoFlow® is one of four practices within AESIT's Form Intelligence pillar, alongside S³ Labs®, IronMind®, and SmartScapes®. Its MEP and BIM coordination work is designed to support gains in the categories the CRELI Index™ uses to score Form Intelligence:

  • Design Integration & Spatial Intelligence — via BIM coordination and cross-discipline clash resolution
  • Sustainability & Human Experience — via energy efficiency and system-level sustainability design
  • Lifecycle Data Continuity — via structured, machine-readable MEP data carried into operations

These are the intended pathways of uplift, not a validated outcome — the CRELI Index™ scoring rubric is still being piloted, and no engagement-level score data exists yet to cite. This page will be updated with real figures once pilot scoring is complete.
