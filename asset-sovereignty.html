01 — The Threat of External Vendor Dependencies

Modern properties are dangerously reliant on cloud-dependent prop-tech providers. When a third-party vendor experiences a service outage or shifts its subscription models, property owners lose access to historical telemetry and core system controls. Asset Sovereignty ensures that all data, network nodes, and operating platforms remain completely owned and hosted inside the physical property footprint.

02 — Structural Sovereignty Policies

1. Isolated Operations: Edge computing systems process critical HVAC, access controls, and power systems locally, running independently of external internet connections.
2. Unrestricted Telemetry Ownership: All sensor datasets are formatted into open, unalterable semantic profiles owned exclusively by the title holder.
