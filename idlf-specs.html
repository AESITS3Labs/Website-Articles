01 — Purpose & Modeling Scope

The Intelligent Design Lifecycle Framework (IDLF®) standardizes the digital genesis of smart buildings. Traditional BIM designs are archived immediately after construction wraps up. The IDLF protocol ensures that the digital model operates as an active, queryable semantic digital twin linked directly to the physical asset during runtime.

02 — Core Engineering Milestones

  1. Semantic Tagging: Mapping all building systems (power distribution, hydronic systems, and building entry points) to standard unified naming rules.
  2. Sensor Array Placement: Integrating sensory paths into load-bearing assemblies, roofing setups, and core mechanical layouts prior to concrete placement.
  3. Lifecycle Pathing: Mapping predictable mechanical replacement windows directly into the spatial coordinates of the 3D model.
